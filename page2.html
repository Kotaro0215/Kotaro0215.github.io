How to make a keyboard

Making a keyboard is easy(Okay maybe it might not be easy),but it just takes time and money.

Step 1 Research what keyboard you want

  • What keyboard size you want
  • Clicky,slient,tactile
  • The budget of your keyboard

    • Step 2 choose a right size of keyboard

  • 96% keyboard includes a num pad
  • Tenkeyless (Keyboard that doesn't include a numpad)
  • 80% that includes pg up and pgdown.
  • 40% (Legacy of iphone keyboard and also the smallest keyboard layout)
  • 60-65% (most common keyboard for custom keyboard users)

    • Step 3 choose what switch you want

    Switches is a must to make a custom keyboard,and it is your choice what type of switch you want.There is 3 types of switches,linear,tactile and clicky.


  • Linear is butterly and smooth switch
  • Tactile makes a tactile bump every keystroke
  • Clicky is just... it just makes a loud noise every keystroke.

    • I would recommand gateron yellow if you are in budget and likes linear switches.

    Step 3 Pick your materials


  • A case
  • PCB that is compatabile with the case
  • A switch
  • Krytox 205,105(optional but recommended)
  • A stab (so it wont rattle)
  • Keycap
